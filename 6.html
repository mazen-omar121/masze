Plantation Monitoring System

Developing a Plantation Monitoring System during industrial training, aspiring engineers undergo rigorous checkpoints to convert theoretical knowledge into practical solutions, preparing them for the challenges of the real-world workspace.

Project Description

The Smart Hidroponik Higrowpot project, initiated at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Tun Aminah, presents an innovative response to the Iskandar Puteri Low Carbon Community Grant offering. With funding and management from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), this endeavor aims to integrate renewable energy and IoT technology into a Nutrient Film Technique hydroponics system. Through industrial training provided by this project, aspiring engineers delve into developing, executing, and testing the monitoring and control IoT system. This immersive experience not only hones technical skills in microcontroller boards and sensor optimization but also fosters vital soft skills like project management and communication. By simulating real-world challenges and emphasizing sustainable solutions, this training equips future engineers with the practical know-how and adaptability needed to thrive in competitive work environments.

Project Requirements

The project requirements for the Smart Hidroponik Higrowpot initiative encompass the integration of renewable energy and IoT technology into a Nutrient Film Technique hydroponics system at Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Tun Aminah. Sponsored and managed by Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), this endeavor aims to address the Iskandar Puteri Low Carbon Community Grant offering's objectives. Specifically, the project necessitates the development, execution, and testing of a monitoring and control IoT system by participants undergoing industrial training. Key requirements include proficiency in microcontroller boards, sensor optimization, project management, and effective communication. Emphasis is placed on sustainability and innovation, reflecting the project's commitment to fostering future engineers equipped to tackle real-world challenges in competitive work environments.
